how mainstream maths education enslaves minds

the mythological equivalent of a cat, a ferengi, and a human walk into a classroom.

the teacher says: "If you follow these steps in this order you will be able to multiply correctly"

the cat multiplies three by two and then declares that the result is seven as she has never liked six so she skipped it; she doesn't care what the teacher thinks

the ferengi notices that by multiplying two times three times two times three times two times three times two times three you get a lot more latinum. she doesn't share this with anyone because she has been taught that women shouldn't have ideas. profits suffer. (a male ferengi also wouldn't share it with anyone but he might profit from it later)

the human notices the cat and the ferengi.
(she also notices a dog who is hastily scribbling an incorrect but adorably close copy of what is on the board into their notes).
the human also has a computer.

`node`, she says to the computer

`Welcome to Node.js v12.18.3.\n Type ".help" for more information.\n >`, the computer replies a vanishingly small number of milliseconds later.

`2 * 3`, she says

`6\n>`

the `teacher` takes the computer and explains that in this class they will learn to do this themselves

no student is allowed to succeed and they never learn who they are
